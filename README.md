# HuKnows

HuKnows is an internal Slack agent that identifies the best people inside an organization to help with a problem and instantly connects you with them.

**The AI that knows who knows.**

---

## 🚩 Problem

In growing organizations, knowledge becomes distributed across teams and conversations.

When someone has a problem, they usually ask:

> "Does anyone know about this?"

This creates friction:

- time lost searching for the right person
- interruptions to the wrong people
- duplicated questions
- slower internal problem solving

HuKnows turns Slack conversations into a **discoverable expertise layer**.

---

## 🧠 What HuKnows does

HuKnows analyzes internal Slack conversations to identify who has the most relevant knowledge about a topic.

When a user asks a question, HuKnows:

1. Searches public Slack channels
2. Detects the most relevant experts
3. Shows the **Top 3 matches**
4. Displays availability
5. Explains _why_ they were selected
6. Instantly connects the user with the expert

---
